Our most recent performance, Bottom Of The Ocean, ran from 2021 through 2022. In BOTO, five visitors are led by three spirit guides through a surreal sequence of invented rituals. Through introspection, catharsis, and connection, the guests dive into the self toward an inevitable destination.

BOTO SOUNDTRACK

Bottom Of The Ocean was, in some ways, an immersive musical. Several songs were written for the performance, and many were sung, call and response, between the audience and the performer. The songs were eventually recorded and released on a 5 song EP.

BOTO GRAPHIC NOVEL

One of the props in BOTO was a 227 page hand drawn diary that revealed the backstory of the performance's resident ghoul, Obsid. Replicas of the diary were published in a limited pressing and sold to fans of the show. Now that Bottom Of The Ocean is closed, a PDF file of Obsid's diary is available to all, downloadable here for free.

One of our favorite documents of BOTO is our lyric video for Tidal Wave Town. This song was originally written for Houseworld and was also prominently featured in Bottom Of The Ocean. Our lyric video is made of footage from equal parts BOTO and Houseworld.

THE VESTIBULE

Each performance of BOTO naturally generated a maximum of four self portraits from the audience. Over the months of presenting Bottom Of The Ocean, our audience created hundreds of self portraits which eventually covered the four walls and ceiling of our vestibule. Click on these images to see hi-res close-ups of the room. If you were one of the visitors to BOTO, try and find your drawing!
