Houseworld

Our first performance, Houseworld, made its debut in 2014. In Houseworld, guests enter an old house and step inside a dream. The basement embodies the underbelly of the psyche, while serenity radiates on the upper floor, and a playfulness inhabits the floors in between. One locked door in the house has never been opened. This door is said to lead to The Light. Due to the arrival of the outsiders, the house's residents believe that maybe tonight is the night that the door to The Light will finally be unlocked.

We are currently reimagining and developing Houseworld. The result will be our next major immersive work.

Bottom Of The Ocean

Bottom Of The Ocean is our most recent performance, opening in 2021 and running for the whole of 2022. In BOTO, an audience of five is guided through a surreal ritual. Through introspection, catharsis, and connection, our visitors dive into the self.

Whisperlodge

Whisperlodge is a live in-person experience of ASMR, the sensory video phenomenon popularized on the internet over the last decade. Co-created by Andrew Hoepfner of Houseworld Immersive and Melinda Lauw, Whisperlodge has presented its soft, intimate journey in New York City, San Francisco, Los Angeles, and Singapore from 2016 to present.

Creator Andrew Hoepfner

Houseworld Immersive is led by Andrew Hoepfner. Hoepfner spent his 20s as a touring musician before falling in love with the form of immersive theater in 2014. Beyond the three major works of Houseworld Immersive, Hoepfner has created an immersive dinner gala for The Future Of Storytelling, immersive installations at Elements Festival, an immersive musical at Ars Nova's Ant Fest, and collaborated on an immersive wedding. As the Artistic Director of Gymnopedie, Hoepfner facilitates the sharing of his Bushwick arts venue with other musicians, fashion designers, dancers, and filmmakers. A sound bath enthusiast, Hoepfner has presented sound baths through his distinctly theatrical series, Waves. He works as an educator in the experiential arts through his workshop, Making Immersive Theater. Hoepfner has collaborated with David Byrne, Young Jean Lee, and Cesar Alvarez.
